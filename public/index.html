Founded in 2014 by Brennan and Leigh Strimple, Rochester Phoenix Martial Arts is a renowned martial arts training facility based in Rochester, NY.

Classes are offered for all levels of skill for both kids and adults. Those with no training are welcome and encouraged to join.

Jui Jitsu training is in the no-gi style. For your first class come wearing athletic clothing, gym shorts and a t-shirt or rashguard. Bring a water bottle, a towel (for sweat) and shoes/sandles to wear off the mat and in the bathroom.
